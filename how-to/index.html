How To Install Color Tables

Watch the video below or follow the step-by-step instructions.

Step-By-Step Instructions (WeatherWise)

1
Download your desired color table and remember where you saved it.
2
In Radar Mode, click the Reflectivity menu at the bottom left.
3
Scroll to the bottom of the menu and select Customize Color Tables.
4
Scroll to the bottom again until you see the Upload Section.
5
Drop your .pal file into the designated upload box or click to browse and upload.
